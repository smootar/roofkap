Our Story

About RoofKAP

A family-owned roofing company built on hard work, honest service, and the belief that every Utah homeowner deserves a roof they can trust.

Who We Are

RoofKAP is a family-owned and operated roofing company serving Utah's Wasatch Front and beyond. We started with a simple idea: deliver the kind of quality work and straightforward service we'd want for our own homes.

That principle hasn't changed. We still answer our own phones, show up when we say we will, and stand behind every roof we install. Our team treats your property with the same care and respect we'd give our own — because our name is on the line every single time.

From emergency leak repairs to full residential and commercial installations, we bring the same attention to detail and commitment to craftsmanship to every project, big or small.
