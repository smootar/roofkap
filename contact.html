Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the questions we hear most often.

Is the inspection really free?

Yes, 100% free with no obligation. We'll assess your roof, document our findings, and give you an honest recommendation — even if the answer is "you don't need anything right now."

Do you help with insurance claims?

Absolutely. We handle the entire process — damage documentation, adjuster meetings, scope preparation, and project management. In most cases, you only pay your deductible.

Do you offer financing?

Yes, we offer flexible financing options so a new roof doesn't have to wait. Contact us for details on available plans and terms.

How fast can you start?

Most projects start within days of approval. Emergency repairs get same-day attention. We work efficiently to minimize disruption to your schedule.

What areas do you serve?

We serve Salt Lake County, Utah County, Davis County, and surrounding communities. Call us to confirm coverage for your specific location.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes. We are fully licensed and carry comprehensive liability and workers' compensation insurance. You're protected from start to finish.
