Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can you respond to a roofing emergency?

We offer 24/7 emergency service and can typically respond within a few hours of your call. Our team will secure your roof to prevent further damage and assess the situation.

Do you provide free estimates?

Yes! We provide free, no-obligation estimates for all of our services. We'll inspect your roof, discuss your options, and provide a detailed written estimate.

Are you licensed and insured?

Absolutely. We are fully licensed and insured roofing contractors in Utah. This protects both our team and your property throughout the project.

What areas do you serve?

We serve Salt Lake County, Utah County, Davis County, and surrounding areas. Contact us to confirm we serve your location.

How long does a typical roof installation take?

Most residential roof installations take 1-3 days depending on the size and complexity of the project. We'll provide a timeline during your estimate.

Do you work with insurance companies?

Yes, we have extensive experience working with insurance companies on storm damage and other claims. We can document damage and provide the necessary information for your claim.
